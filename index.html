Common Questions

How is YUMO different from a recipe app?

Recipe apps are passive databases. YUMO is an active Intelligence OS. It doesn't wait for you to search; it analyzes your life context and provides the answer before you even ask.

Does it work with my specific diet?

Yes. Whether you are Vegan, Keto, Halal, or have severe nut allergies, YUMO’s core engine filters every single intelligence layer through your safety and preference profile.

Is my personal data safe?

Your food memories and habits are private. We use bank-grade encryption and never sell your personal data to advertisers. Our business model is based on intelligence, not ads.
